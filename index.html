› 5 BIASES · CLICK CARD TO REVEAL FIX
B/01
Home Bias
Living in Bulgaria creates a structural pull toward over-weighting local assets — suboptimal for true diversification.
PORTFOLIO FIX
IWDA (25%) + EIMI (8%) enforce global reach across 23 developed + 24 emerging markets. Bulgarian exposure capped at BULGAR 4%.
IPS R02 · Max 25% per instrument
REVEAL FIX
B/02
Loss Aversion
Losses feel ~2× more painful than equivalent gains (Kahneman & Tversky, 1979). Risk of panic-selling in a drawdown.
PORTFOLIO FIX
30-day behavioral guard prevents strategic changes after a >10% market decline. €15k cash buffer removes urgency to sell.
IPS R04 · 30-day blackout post -10%
REVEAL FIX
B/03
Overconfidence
17 years in IT may tempt over-allocating to technology — "I know this domain better than most investors."
PORTFOLIO FIX
XDWT hard-capped at 5%. Zero individual tech stock positions. All equity exposure passive and index-based.
IPS · No sector >5% of portfolio
REVEAL FIX
B/04
Recency Bias
Extrapolating recent winners into the future — chasing last year's best-performing asset class.
PORTFOLIO FIX
Annual rebalancing on 30 April mechanically enforces "buy low, sell high" — overrides momentum by selling outperformers.
IPS R03 · Annual rebalance 30 Apr
REVEAL FIX
B/05
Anchoring
Refusing to rebalance because a position is "down from what I paid" — prioritising cost price over target weight.
PORTFOLIO FIX
Rebalancing targets are percentage weights — the system is deliberately indifferent to purchase price.
Weight-based · not price-based
REVEAL FIX
